Biography

Hello! I'm Winston Sullivan, an enthusiastic photographer with 8 years of experience in capturing the essence of urban landscapes and the beauty of natural sceneries. My journey in photography began during my time in Vienna, Austria, which ignited my passion for this art form. Living in Portland, Maine, I am constantly inspired by the surrounding vistas and dynamic cityscapes.

Photography Approach

Specializing in urban and landscape photography, I've completed several paid projects, displaying my skills in handheld and drone photography/videography. I've worked with my trusted Canon cameras, currently on my 5th, and have an impressive collection of Canon EF lenses. Each piece helps me bring a unique perspective to my work. Besides photography, I have a strong affinity for technology and developed this website myself.
